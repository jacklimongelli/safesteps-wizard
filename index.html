4. EXECUTION
HOW IT WORKS
PHASE I

Assessment & Floor Plan

Complete a short intake, then scan your home using your iPhone's LiDAR technology and send us the floor plan. Prefer we handle it? We'll come to you. Either way, your plan starts with your actual space.

PHASE II

Plan Build

We take your intake and floor plan and build a dynamic scenario response plan — rally points, decision trees, and role-specific instructions for every person in your home.

PHASE III

Briefing & Training

We walk your family through the plan start to finish. Families who want to go deeper can add in-person training — scenario walkthroughs, stress inoculation, and live drills in your actual home.

PHASE IV

Rehearsal

The plan only works if you've run it. Use our online portal to simulate emergency situations and track rehearsal sessions, after-action notes, and equipment checks — so your family builds real proficiency over time, not just paper preparedness.
