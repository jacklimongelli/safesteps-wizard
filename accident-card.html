Steps to Follow
Check injuries. Don't move anyone with neck/back pain. Call 911 if hurt.
Hazard lights on. Move to shoulder if drivable. Do not leave the scene.
Call 911 if injuries, major damage, or blocking traffic. "Accident at _______ & _______, near _______. There are / are not injuries."
No fault admission. Facts only — time, location, what you saw.
Exchange info with other driver. Fill in fields on Side 2.
Get witnesses — names and phones before they leave.
Photograph everything. See checklist on Side 2.
Officer info: name, badge #, how to get the police report.
Call your insurance — even if not at fault. Report within 24–72 hrs. Car undrivable? Call insurance and ask if they cover a tow and have a preferred body shop. Give that body shop name to the tow truck driver.
See a doctor. Whiplash and internal injuries may not appear for hours. After: keep all medical bills, treatment records, and repair estimates for your claim.
